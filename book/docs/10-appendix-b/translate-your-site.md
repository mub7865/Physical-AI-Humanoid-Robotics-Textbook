# Community & Resources ü§ù

Robotics is hard, but you don't have to do it alone. Here are the best places to find help, documentation, and fellow engineers.

## Official Documentation üìö

Always read the docs first.
* **ROS 2 Humble:** [docs.ros.org/en/humble](https://docs.ros.org/en/humble/)
* **NVIDIA Isaac Sim:** [docs.omniverse.nvidia.com/app_isaacsim](https://docs.omniverse.nvidia.com/app_isaacsim)
* **Nav2:** [navigation.ros.org](https://navigation.ros.org/)
* **Gazebo:** [gazebosim.org/docs](https://gazebosim.org/docs)

## Forums & Support üí¨

* **Robotics Stack Exchange:** The "StackOverflow" for robots.
    * [robotics.stackexchange.com](https://robotics.stackexchange.com/)
* **ROS Discourse:** Announcements and discussions about the future of ROS.
    * [discourse.ros.org](https://discourse.ros.org/)
* **NVIDIA Developer Forums:** For Jetson and Isaac issues.
    * [forums.developer.nvidia.com](https://forums.developer.nvidia.com/)

## YouTube Channels to Watch üì∫

* **The Construct:** Great tutorials on ROS 2 and Gazebo.
